About Us

Kangemi FreshGrain Cereals is a locally based cereals supplier serving Kangemi, Mountain View, Kinoo, Westlands, and surrounding areas. We sell both retail and wholesale, supplying households, dukas, hotels, schools, and food vendors.

We source directly from farmers and trusted rural markets to keep prices fair and quality consistent throughout the year.
